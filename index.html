passionate and committed

Over 60 dedicated staff members

At Sunrise Care Centre, over 60 skilled professionals are committed to delivering exceptional care and service to our residents. Each member of our team brings a wealth of expertise and a genuine passion for enhancing the lives of our seniors. We pride ourselves on creating a warm and supportive environment where residents feel valued and respected every day.

Our staff’s dedication goes beyond mere caregiving; it encompasses a holistic approach that prioritizes physical, mental, and emotional well-being. By fostering strong, compassionate relationships with our residents, we ensure that everyone at Sunrise experiences a sense of belonging and family. Discover the difference that our committed team makes in providing top-notch care in a vibrant and nurturing community.

facilities available

Your wellness is our priority

Weekly Excercise

Stay active and healthy with our regular exercise sessions tailored to all fitness levels. Our weekly exercise programs include a variety of activities designed to promote physical well-being, enhance mobility, and boost overall energy.

Social events

Engage in a vibrant social life with our diverse range of activities. From arts and crafts, bingo, and puzzle building to Saturday movies, music concert afternoons, and Friday Pub & Braai Nights, there's always something happening at Sunrise.

Medical support

Benefit from comprehensive medical support services right on site. Our dedicated team includes a resident doctor, audiologist, physiotherapist, and wound care specialist, ensuring all your health needs are met with professional expertise.
